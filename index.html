Langkawi, Malaysia

Langkawi is a district and an archipelago of 99 islands in the Andaman Sea some 30 km off the mainland coast of northwestern Malaysia. Every year tens of thousands of visitors visit Langkawi which also has the status of duty-free shoppings. Kuah is the largest city and Cenang Beach is one of the popular beaches in Langkawi.
